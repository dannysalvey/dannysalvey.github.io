Ensembles

The Pennsylvania State University Marching Blue Band:

I started marching for the Blue Band in 2022, after a virtual music audition and an in-person marching audition. The audition process was definitely nerve-racking, as all prospective members had to wait to hear their name called in front of the entire band. In 2025-2026, I was the Center Snare, leading the entire band by starting each song by "tapping off", keeping tempo, and providing valuable feedback for other drumline members to continue to improve their skills!

The George Mason University Indoor Drumline:

I am currently in my second year with the ensemble, starting with my audition in the fall of 2024. GMU is a highly competitive ensemble, competing in Winter Guard International (WGI) in the Percussion Independent World Class (PIW) division. Last year we placed 6th at finals, with a score of 94.863 as well as achieving the fan favorite show for the year! This year we are working with the momentum we built last year to continually improve and provide fans with the best product possible.

Lessons

I offer music lessons in the Greater Philadelphia Area, as well as virtual lessons through Zoom. I specialize in marching snare drum and percussion fundamentals. My goal is to encapsulate all necessary information to improve as a musician within 60-90 minute lessons, focusing on technique, approach, and fundamentals. I offer flexible, competitive pricing.

For more information, please contact me through either email or text. Please include your full name and tell me that you are interested in lessons!
